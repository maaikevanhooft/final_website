DESIGN JUSTIFICATIONS

 

FLOW DIAGRAM

The flow diagram shows that the main page contains a navigation, with 5 pages about Breda Tunes and 1 page about the design justifications. The navigation ensures that all pages can be reached. Each page shows which 'column grid' has been used. All pages are responsive, so they can be used on all desktop sizes and mobile phones.
You can also see where special features have been used, such as different buttons and a carousel.

FIRST PROTOTYPE (including feedback)

HOMEPAGE

Feedback:

The text 'HOME' can be changed to another title. So that it piques the interest of the users of the website to discover the whole website, instead of just the home page.

A CALL TO ACTION to buy tickets is required! After seeing this website, it is important that tickets can be purchased immediately.

More information may be given about the artists. Now you only see the name, but it is interesting to know what their expertise is.

A 'contact us' button is missing.

ABOUT US

Feedback:

The 'Contact Us' button is also missing at this page.

The vision is not entirely clear, so this must be added.

SPRING EDITION

Feedback:

The 'Contact Us' button is also missing at this page.

On this page of the website it is also interesting to show the 'MAP' of the festival site. This is something visitors want to know when they look at the website, so that they know where the artists are performing and where they can park the car, for example.

AFTER TALKS

Feedback:

The 'Contact Us' button is also missing at this page.

Someone gave the tip to show star reviews on this page of the website, so that visitors can see at a glance whether the festival is well appreciated or not. With the piece 'YOU AS OUR PHOTOGRAPHER' it is interesting to show more than 1 photo, so that visitors can see what kind of photos they can submit.

TEAM

Feedback:

The 'Contact Us' button is also missing at this page.

Now there are only the names of the staff. As a visitor it is more interesting to be able to read who they are and what they find important in their work.

FONT NAMES AND COLOUR CODES

For the design of the website, the photos taken at the Jazz festival have been carefully considered. A photo is taken as inspiration, where the colors are taken from. These colors form the basis for the entire website and they convey the atmosphere of the Jazz festival well.

The colors ensure that the studio lights of the Jazz performance form the basis for the website.




The fonts used are all easy to read. They match the simplistic design of the website and give it a professional look.
