THE JAZZ FESTIVAL 2021

After last year's successful edition, in May 2021 it will be time again for the annual Jazz Festival in Breda!
Come and enjoy the most beautiful music with friends and family at Breda Tunes.

Get your tickets for 20 and 21 May, to look forward to the best party of the year!

This year's artists

Berry Summer

With his professional expertise in trumpet, with his personal song 'Fade Away' he blows everyone away. Listen and relax...

Tom West

Tom is 23 years old, the youngest Jazz Artist at Breda Tunes. He is known for his fantastic performances with the saxophone. Nobody can do that the way Tom can.

Alex Jackson

Alex only plays his own written songs in this edition. We get a look at his own expertise. With his own and unique style, he surprises everyone.

Sanny Simons

Sanny lets us take a look at today's modern Jazz. She shows us that Jazz can also be played with the electric guitar. She introduces the public to her modern twist.

 

LINE UP

The line up of this special edition in 2021 will remain a secret for a while. But, a special edition also comes with a special line-up! We can already reveal that Berry Summer, Tom West, Alex Jackson and Sunny Simons will be there.
When you know these four artists, you can no longer pass up the tickets ... can you?
