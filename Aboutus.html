ABOUT US

 

JAZZ IS A WAY OF LIFE

At Breda Tunes we breathe music. And specifically Jazz. That is why we want to share our passion for jazz with the whole world, in the form of a festival. One of the best festivals in the Netherlands. We think it is very important that Breda Tunes gives a stage to young talents, to let them grow into the good artists they are inside. In addition, we think it is very important that we offer Jazz music for all ages.

Our vision is to enjoy great music with the whole family.
